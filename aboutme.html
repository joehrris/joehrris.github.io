About Me

Hello! I'm Joe Harris, a passionate teacher, coding enthusiast, and storyteller. I created this space to share my experiences, projects, and thoughts with the world. I graduated from NTU with a 2:1 in Bsc Computer Science in 2024 and began my PGCE / QTS training around 3 months later.

When I'm not teaching or coding, you might find me exploring new hobbies, reading books, or simply enjoying some downtime by playing some games. I believe in the power of creativity and curiosity to transform lives and that we as educators have a responability to nurture this.

This portfolio showcases some of my projects and blog posts that reflect my journey, challenges, and successes. Thank you for stopping by!
